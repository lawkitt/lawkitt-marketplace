## 1. Document Completeness

## 4. Deliverables & Specifications

**Check for Skala elements:**
- [ ] Content type defined (posts, stories, videos, reels)
- [ ] Platforms specified (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, etc.)
- [ ] Quantity clear
- [ ] Campaign Start Date
- [ ] Posting schedule/deadlines

### Quality & Approval
- [ ] Brand approval right defined
- [ ] Revision limits stated (2-3 typical)

## 5. Compensation

- [ ] Fee amount clear

## 6. Intellectual Property

**Check for Skala standard:**
> "Influencer retains ownership and grants Brand non-exclusive, worldwide, perpetual, royalty-free, sublicensable license"

- [ ] Ownership: who retains?
- [ ] License type: exclusive or non-exclusive
- [ ] Territory: worldwide
- [ ] Duration: time-limited or perpetual
- [ ] Sublicense rights: yes/no
- [ ] Use scope: marketing, promotion, commercial

### Preexisting/Third-Party IP
- [ ] Addressed in agreement
- [ ] License grant to Brand
- [ ] Influencer must identify third-party materials
- [ ] Ensure permissions obtained
