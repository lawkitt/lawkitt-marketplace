# Corporate & Formation Documents Checklist

## Formation Documents

### Certificate of Incorporation
- [ ] Original Certificate of Incorporation
- [ ] All Certificates of Amendment
- [ ] Current Restated Certificate (if any)
- [ ] Certificate of Designation (for preferred stock series)

**Review for:**
- Authorized shares (common and preferred)
- Par value
- Protective provisions
- Board composition requirements
- Registered agent current

### Bylaws
- [ ] Current Bylaws
- [ ] All Bylaw amendments
- [ ] Confirm consistency with Certificate

**Review for:**
- Board size and composition
- Officer roles and authority
- Meeting/notice requirements
- Indemnification provisions
- Amendment procedures

### Good Standing
- [ ] Certificate of Good Standing (state of incorporation)
- [ ] Foreign qualification certificates (states where doing business)
- [ ] Franchise tax receipts

## Corporate Records

### Board Materials
- [ ] All Board meeting minutes
- [ ] Written consents in lieu of meetings
- [ ] Board resolutions (financing, equity grants, officers)

**Key resolutions to verify:**
- Formation/organization
- Officer appointments
- Stock issuances (each round)
- Option grants and 409A valuations
- SAFE/convertible note authorizations
- Material contracts approval
- Bank account authorizations

### Stockholder Materials
- [ ] All Stockholder meeting minutes
- [ ] Written consents in lieu of meetings
- [ ] Stockholder resolutions

**Key approvals to verify:**
- Charter amendments
- Stock issuances (if required)
- Equity incentive plan adoption
- Major transactions

### Organizational Documents
- [ ] Organizational chart (legal entities)
- [ ] List of all subsidiaries (if any)
- [ ] Subsidiary formation documents
- [ ] Intercompany agreements

## Officers and Directors

- [ ] Current list of directors with appointment dates
- [ ] Current list of officers with appointment dates
- [ ] Director/Officer resignation letters (if any)
- [ ] D&O questionnaires

## State Compliance

- [ ] Annual report filings (all states)
- [ ] Franchise tax payments (Delaware)
- [ ] State tax registrations
- [ ] Business licenses and permits
