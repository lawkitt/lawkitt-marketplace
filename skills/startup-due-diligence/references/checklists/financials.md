# Financial Due Diligence Checklist

## Financial Statements

### Historical Financials
- [ ] Income statements (2-3 years, or since inception)
- [ ] Balance sheets
- [ ] Cash flow statements
- [ ] Notes to financials

**Review for:**
- Revenue recognition policies
- Expense categorization
- Consistency across periods
- Accounting method (cash vs. accrual)

### Interim Financials
- [ ] Year-to-date financials
- [ ] Monthly financials (trailing 12 months)
- [ ] Comparison to prior periods

### Audit Status
- [ ] Audited financials (if any)
- [ ] Review engagement reports
- [ ] Audit management letters
- [ ] Auditor independence

## Revenue Analysis

### Revenue Breakdown
- [ ] Revenue by product/service
- [ ] Revenue by customer
- [ ] Revenue by geography
- [ ] MRR/ARR analysis (SaaS)

### Revenue Quality
- [ ] Revenue recognition policy
- [ ] Deferred revenue schedule
- [ ] Backlog/pipeline
- [ ] Customer contracts (for revenue verification)

**SaaS Metrics:**
- MRR/ARR growth
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
- Gross Revenue Retention
- Churn rate
- Average Contract Value (ACV)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)

### Customer Analysis
- [ ] Customer count and growth
- [ ] Customer concentration
- [ ] Cohort analysis
- [ ] Churn analysis

## Expense Analysis

### Operating Expenses
- [ ] Cost of goods sold / Cost of revenue
- [ ] R&D expenses
- [ ] Sales and marketing expenses
- [ ] General and administrative expenses

### Payroll
- [ ] Payroll summary
- [ ] Headcount by department
- [ ] Compensation benchmarking

### Capital Expenditures
- [ ] CapEx history
- [ ] Fixed asset schedule
- [ ] Depreciation schedules

## Cash & Treasury

### Cash Position
- [ ] Bank statements (3-6 months)
- [ ] Cash reconciliation
- [ ] Restricted cash

### Cash Management
- [ ] Cash burn rate
- [ ] Runway calculation
- [ ] Cash forecast

### Debt
- [ ] Schedule of indebtedness
- [ ] Loan agreements
- [ ] Payment history

## Tax

### Tax Returns
- [ ] Federal tax returns (3 years)
- [ ] State tax returns
- [ ] International tax returns (if applicable)

### Tax Positions
- [ ] Net operating losses (NOL)
- [ ] R&D tax credits
- [ ] Tax provision analysis
- [ ] Transfer pricing (if international)

### Payroll Tax
- [ ] 941 filings
- [ ] State payroll filings
- [ ] Payroll tax deposits

### Sales Tax
- [ ] Sales tax returns
- [ ] Nexus analysis
- [ ] Sales tax compliance

## Working Capital

### Accounts Receivable
- [ ] A/R aging
- [ ] Bad debt reserve
- [ ] Collection history

### Accounts Payable
- [ ] A/P aging
- [ ] Payment terms
- [ ] Accrued expenses

### Inventory (if applicable)
- [ ] Inventory listing
- [ ] Inventory valuation method
- [ ] Obsolescence reserve

## Projections

### Financial Model
- [ ] Revenue projections
- [ ] Expense projections
- [ ] Cash flow projections
- [ ] Key assumptions

### Budget
- [ ] Current year budget
- [ ] Budget vs. actual variance

## Controls & Processes

### Financial Controls
- [ ] Accounting policies
- [ ] Approval authorities
- [ ] Segregation of duties
- [ ] Bank account signatories

### Systems
- [ ] Accounting software
- [ ] Billing/invoicing system
- [ ] Payroll system
- [ ] Expense management
