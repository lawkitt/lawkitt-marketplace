# Material Contracts Checklist

## Customer Contracts

### Sales Agreements
- [ ] Customer contracts / MSAs
- [ ] Order forms / SOWs
- [ ] Terms of Service (online)
- [ ] SLAs

**Review for:**
- Revenue terms and payment
- Term and renewal provisions
- Termination rights (especially for convenience)
- Liability caps and indemnities
- Assignment/change of control provisions
- Most Favored Nation (MFN) clauses
- Exclusivity provisions

### Key Customer Analysis
- [ ] Top 10 customers by revenue
- [ ] Customer concentration analysis
- [ ] Churn data
- [ ] Contract renewal dates

**Flag if:**
- Single customer >25% of revenue
- Top 3 customers >50% of revenue
- Key contracts expiring soon
- Unusual termination rights

## Vendor/Supplier Contracts

### Key Vendors
- [ ] Cloud hosting agreements (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- [ ] Software licenses
- [ ] SaaS subscriptions
- [ ] Hardware/equipment agreements

**Review for:**
- Pricing and payment terms
- Term and auto-renewal
- Data ownership and portability
- Termination provisions
- SLA commitments

### Professional Services
- [ ] Legal services engagement
- [ ] Accounting/audit engagement
- [ ] Consulting agreements
- [ ] Marketing/PR agency agreements

## Strategic Agreements

### Partnership Agreements
- [ ] Strategic partnership agreements
- [ ] Channel partner agreements
- [ ] Reseller agreements
- [ ] Integration agreements

**Review for:**
- Exclusivity provisions
- Non-compete restrictions
- Revenue sharing
- IP licensing terms
- Term and termination

### Joint Ventures
- [ ] JV agreements
- [ ] Operating agreements
- [ ] Contribution agreements

## Financial Contracts

### Debt Agreements
- [ ] Bank loan agreements
- [ ] Lines of credit
- [ ] Equipment financing
- [ ] Venture debt agreements

**Review for:**
- Principal and interest
- Maturity dates
- Covenants (financial and operational)
- Default provisions
- Change of control triggers
- Warrants or equity kickers

### Guarantees
- [ ] Personal guarantees by founders
- [ ] Corporate guarantees
- [ ] Letters of credit

## Real Estate

### Leases
- [ ] Office lease agreements
- [ ] Lease amendments
- [ ] Sublease agreements

**Review for:**
- Term and renewal options
- Rent and escalations
- Security deposits
- Assignment/subletting rights
- Personal guarantees
- Termination provisions

## Insurance

### Policies
- [ ] General liability insurance
- [ ] D&O insurance
- [ ] E&O / Professional liability
- [ ] Cyber liability
- [ ] Workers compensation
- [ ] Property insurance

**Verify:**
- Coverage amounts adequate
- Named insureds correct
- Policy current and premiums paid
- Notice requirements for claims

## Other Material Contracts

### Government Contracts
- [ ] Federal contracts
- [ ] State/local contracts
- [ ] Compliance certifications

### Regulatory Agreements
- [ ] Consent decrees
- [ ] Settlement agreements
- [ ] Compliance agreements

### Related Party Transactions
- [ ] Contracts with founders/officers
- [ ] Contracts with affiliates
- [ ] Loans to/from related parties

**Scrutinize carefully for:**
- Arm's length terms
- Board approval
- Proper disclosure

## Change of Control Analysis

Review all material contracts for change of control provisions:
- [ ] Contracts requiring consent for assignment
- [ ] Contracts terminating on change of control
- [ ] Contracts with acceleration provisions
- [ ] Contracts with MFN triggered by financing
