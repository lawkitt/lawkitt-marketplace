## United States

### Delaware C-Corp
- **Cost:** from $750
- **Registration time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 21% federal corporate tax
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$1,900
- **Best for:** VC-backed startups, SaaS, AI, GameDev, Space
- **Key benefits:** Industry standard for fundraising, well-established legal framework, investor-familiar, flexible equity structuring (SAFEs, priced rounds, stock options)
- **Market share:** ~80% of US public companies are Delaware corporations

### Delaware Public Benefit Corporation (PBC)
- **Cost:** from $750
- **Registration time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 21% federal corporate tax
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$1,900
- **Best for:** AI startups with mission focus, social enterprises
- **Key benefits:** Allows directors to consider public benefit alongside shareholder profits, attracts mission-aligned investors, signals commitment to broader societal impact
- **Notable users:** Anthropic; OpenAI announced restructuring to PBC (November 2024)

### Delaware LLC
- **Cost:** from $750
- **Registration time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 0-37% (pass-through to owner's personal tax return)
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$1,800
- **Best for:** Solopreneurs, SPVs, consultants, non-VC-backed businesses
- **Key benefits:** Pass-through taxation (no corporate-level tax), simpler structure, profits/losses flow to owner's personal return
- **Note:** Gold standard for onshore SPVs due to flexible legal framework

### Wyoming C-Corp
- **Cost:** from $750
- **Registration time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 21% federal (no state corporate income tax)
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$1,500
- **Best for:** Bootstrapped teams seeking privacy and cost efficiency
- **Key benefits:** Anonymous ownership (shareholders/directors not disclosed in public filings), lowest maintenance costs, strong privacy protections

### Wyoming LLC
- **Cost:** from $750
- **Registration time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 0-37% (pass-through)
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$1,500
- **Best for:** Asset holding, real estate, intellectual property protection
- **Key benefits:** No state income/estate/inheritance taxes, strong liability protection, no public disclosure of members/managers

### Wyoming DAO LLC
- **Cost:** from $1,000
- **Registration time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 0-37% (pass-through)
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$1,500
- **Best for:** DAOs, decentralized organizations with for-profit activities
- **Key benefits:** Legal recognition of smart contract governance, limited liability protection for members
- **Market data:** 1,000+ entities with "DAO LLC" registered in Wyoming as of February 2025
- **Legal basis:** Wyoming DAO Supplement (2021)

### Texas C-Corp
- **Cost:** from $750
- **Registration time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 21% federal corporate tax
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$1,400
- **Best for:** AI startups, Space startups, founders seeking Delaware alternatives
- **Key benefits:** Corporate-friendly environment, predictable judiciary, lower state-level taxes, emerging as alternative to Delaware following recent case law shifts
- **Notable users:** x.AI (Elon Musk's AI company)

### Texas LLC
- **Cost:** from $750
- **Registration time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 0-37% (pass-through)
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$1,400
- **Best for:** Solopreneurs, influencers, Texas-based businesses
- **Key benefits:** No state income tax, business-friendly environment

### Nevada C-Corp
- **Cost:** from $750
- **Registration time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 21% federal corporate tax
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$2,000
- **Best for:** AI startups, Space startups, privacy-focused founders
- **Key benefits:** No state corporate income tax, strong privacy protections, no information-sharing agreement with IRS

### Nevada LLC
- **Cost:** from $750
- **Registration time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 0-37% (pass-through)
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$1,800
- **Best for:** Asset holding, privacy-focused businesses
- **Key benefits:** Strong liability protection, anonymous ownership, only state without IRS information-sharing agreement

### California C-Corp
- **Cost:** from $750
- **Registration time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 21% federal + ~8.84% state
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$2,200
- **Best for:** Tech/entertainment companies with California presence
- **Key benefits:** Proximity to Silicon Valley investors and tech ecosystem
- **Drawback:** Higher state taxes and regulatory requirements

### California LLC
- **Cost:** from $750
- **Registration time:** 2-3 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 0-37% (pass-through) + state taxes
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$2,200
- **Best for:** Local California businesses, entrepreneurs operating within California
- **Drawback:** Higher taxes and more regulatory requirements than other states

## Offshore Jurisdictions

### British Virgin Islands (BVI) Limited Company
- **Cost:** from $5,500
- **Registration time:** 3-4 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 0%
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$2,300
- **Best for:** Token issuance, offshore SPVs, asset holding, crypto projects, proprietary trading
- **Key benefits:** Zero corporate tax, no licensing required for single token issuance (per 2023 BVI crypto regulation), strong privacy, flexible corporate structure
- **Notable users:** Many CoinList projects use BVI entities
- **Common uses:** Token-issuing entities for crypto projects, offshore SPVs, investment holding structures

### Panama Limited Company (LTD)
- **Cost:** from $2,900
- **Registration time:** 3-4 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 0% (on foreign-source income)
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$1,400
- **Best for:** Crypto startups, web3 GameDev (especially Telegram mini apps), multi-issuance token projects
- **Key benefits:** No specific crypto regulation (as of March 2025), territorial tax system (foreign income not taxed), allows multiple token issuances with minimal restrictions
- **Notable users:** Polymarket, SushiSwap
- **Regulatory status:** Removed from FATF gray list in October 2023 after strengthening AML/CTF measures

### UAE Free Zone Company (FZCO)
- **Cost:** from $6,000
- **Registration time:** 3-4 weeks
- **Tax rate:** 0%
- **Annual maintenance:** ~$7,300
- **Best for:** Crypto startups targeting Middle East, space ventures with UAE ties, businesses seeking zero-tax jurisdiction with regional presence
- **Key benefits:** Zero corporate and personal income tax, growing crypto ecosystem, UAE government support for space industry
- **Drawbacks:** Higher annual maintenance costs, crypto regulations have been introduced in certain emirates
- **Note:** Best suited for projects with Middle East market focus or UAE investor relationships

## Common Multi-Entity Structures

### Crypto Project (Typical Structure)
1. **Delaware C-Corp** (DevCo/LabsCo)
   - Purpose: Protocol development, VC fundraising, employment contracts
   - Instruments: SAFE notes, Token Warrants, stock options
2. **BVI or Panama LTD** (Token Issuer)
   - Purpose: Token generation and distribution
   - BVI for single issuance; Panama for multiple issuances
3. **Panama Foundation** (Optional - for mature projects)
   - Purpose: Ecosystem grants, treasury management
   - Recommended only for Top 200 CoinMarketCap projects

### International SaaS
1. **Delaware C-Corp** - US operations, VC fundraising
2. **Local subsidiaries** - Regional operations as needed for compliance or market presence

### Solopreneur (Non-US Tax Resident)
1. **Delaware LLC** - US business operations
2. Personal tax obligations determined by founder's tax residency
3. Benefit from pass-through taxation where LLC itself pays no tax

### SPV Structure Options
- **Onshore (US investors):** Delaware LLC or Delaware Series LLC
- **Offshore:** BVI Limited Company
- **Multiple investments:** Delaware Series LLC (segregates liabilities between series)
